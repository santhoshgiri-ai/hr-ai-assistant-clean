EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

Working Hours
The standard working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are expected to complete 8 working hours per day.

Notice Period
The standard notice period for employees is 60 days.
Employees must inform their manager and HR before resignation.

Work From Home Policy
Employees are allowed to work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and follow company ethics.

Leave Application Process
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by the reporting manager.

IT and Security Policy
Employees must not share company confidential data with external parties.
Company laptops should be used only for official purposes.

Equal Opportunity Policy
The company provides equal opportunity to all employees regardless of gender, race, religion, or nationality.
